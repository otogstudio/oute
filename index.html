OUTE®

English-made caps shaped by older ways of doing things.

OUTE® is a small collection of caps that take their cues from heritage English headwear. Each design starts with archival shapes and proportions, then gets reworked into something intended for everyday wear.

The materials are sourced in Britain where possible, and every cap is cut and sewn in England. We work in small runs, take our time with the details, and only release what we believe in.
