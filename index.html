This cap takes its shape from traditional cycling and cricket caps, reworked into something more casual and everyday. It's constructed in the old way, fully lined inside, and cut from a heavier weight cotton twill.

It ages beautifully. The cloth softens, the colours settle, and the cap becomes entirely yours over time. Made in London.

Each button is pressed into sand by hand, with the stones set in place before casting. Molten pewter is poured into the mould and left to set. Once cooled, each piece is finished and polished by hand.

The stones are lab-grown, chemically identical to their natural counterparts. Lead-free English pewter, 2cm diameter. Made to replace the top button on a favourite shirt or jacket, or anywhere you see fit.
